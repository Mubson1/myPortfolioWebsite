Islington College

Islington college is an educational institution situated in Kathmandu, Nepal. The college website of this institution is attractive and the feature I like the most is the toggling of pictures in its homepage. It is unique and provides visitors to switch the images instead of placing lots of pictures in a single webpaege. Other webpages can be loaded with tons of pictures but a home page doesn't look good when there are lots of pictures.

This technique of adding toggle feature in the webpage for changing the pictures is quite impressive.The home page of this website also includes similiar feature where visitors can change the pictures through toggle button. Background of the article section contains switchable images and a small description on it.
